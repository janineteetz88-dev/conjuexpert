Every morning, Clara sits at her small kitchen table, sipping coffee while the sun streams through the window. Yesterday, she sat there, lost in thought, as the world outside buzzed with life. Her cat, Max, always finds a spot on her lap, purring softly as she strokes his fur. Last week, they sat together in silence, watching the raindrops race down the glass.
