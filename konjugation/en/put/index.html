Geschichte mit „put"

As the sun dipped below the horizon, Maria put her keys on the kitchen counter and sighed. She had put off cooking dinner, opting instead for takeout. Just then, her daughter Ella came in, her backpack slung over one shoulder. "Can we put on some music?" she asked eagerly. Maria smiled and put her phone on the speaker, filling the room with soft melodies. They danced together, forgetting the day's worries, as the last light faded and night embraced them.
Als die Sonne hinter dem Horizont verschwand, legte Maria ihre Schlüssel auf die Küchentheke und seufzte. Sie hatte das Kochen des Abendessens aufgeschoben und sich stattdessen für Takeout entschieden.
