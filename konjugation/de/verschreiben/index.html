Während ich auf das neue Rezept wartete, dachte ich darüber nach, wie wichtig es ist, genau zu sein. Schließlich bekam ich das richtige Medikament und konnte endlich nach Hause gehen.
The doctor prescribed me a new medication to relieve my headaches. When I entered the pharmacy, I noticed that the prescription had been miswritten: instead of painkillers, the doctor had prescribed antibiotics. I explained the mistake to the pharmacist, and she immediately called the practice. The doctor apologized and quickly prescribed me the correct medication. While I waited for the new prescription, I thought about how important it is to be precise. Finally, I got the right medication and could finally go home.
